Crustal magnetization of Mercury

This project investigates Mercury's ancient crustal anomalies and finds that they require a stronger ancient dynamo. Our results suggest that the core was subject to a heightened energy flux powering this strong dynamo prior to at least ~3.7 billion years ago. Further, our results make testable predictions for the properties of the strongest magnetization in the Hermean surface which can be further investigated by the upcoming BepiColombo mission.
