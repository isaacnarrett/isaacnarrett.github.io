Impact plasma amplification of the ancient Mercury magnetic field

Building on our lunar dynamo work, this project extends impact plasma amplification models to Mercury's ancient magnetic field. We investigate how large impact events on Mercury could have generated transient plasma environments capable of amplifying and preserving signatures of the planet's early dynamo activity. This research contributes to our understanding of how impact processes shaped the magnetic evolution of terrestrial planets, providing crucial insights for interpreting MESSENGER magnetometer data and preparing for future BepiColombo observations.
