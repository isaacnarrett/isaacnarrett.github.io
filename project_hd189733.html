Axisymmetric High Spot Coverage on Exoplanet Host HD 189733 A

This project characterizes the spottedness of HD 189733 A to explore the "transit light source" effect. We found that HD 189733 A is best described as ~30-40% spotted, highlighting the importance of accounting for stellar heterogeneity in transmission spectroscopy.
